Programs & Partnerships

Experiential learning opportunities and strategic collaborations

Experiential Learning at Scale

We design and deliver comprehensive programs that bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, providing students with real-world exposure and practical skills.

Industrial Visits (IVs)

Our curated industrial visits offer students a firsthand look at how theoretical concepts are applied in real-world settings. These visits are designed to:

  • Bridge the gap between classroom learning and industrial practices
  • Provide insight into industrial processes and technologies
  • Expose students to professional work environments
  • Facilitate interaction with industry experts

We coordinate visits to leading companies across various sectors, ensuring relevance to students' academic backgrounds and career aspirations.

Schedule an Industrial Visit

In-Plant Trainings (IPTs)

Our In-Plant Training programs provide immersive learning experiences within industrial settings, offering:

  • Hands-on experience with industrial equipment and processes
  • Practical application of theoretical knowledge
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Exposure to real-world problem-solving scenarios

These trainings are tailored to specific academic disciplines and can range from short-term modules to comprehensive programs spanning several weeks.

Domestic & International Internship Coordination

We facilitate internship placements for students across domestic and international organizations, providing:

  • Access to a diverse network of partner companies
  • Customized matching based on skills and career goals
  • Comprehensive pre-internship orientation
  • Ongoing support throughout the internship period
  • Post-internship reflection and skill integration

Our international internships offer additional cultural exposure and global perspective, enhancing students' adaptability and cross-cultural competence.
