Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Acadetours" mean?

The name "Acadetours" combines "Academia" and "Detour" â€” representing our mission to guide students on a purposeful detour from conventional theory-driven education toward applied, practical learning journeys. We're an educational enabler bridging the gap between academics and industry.

How can educational institutions partner with Acadetours?

Educational institutions can partner with us through various models, including digital learning enablement, campus placement support, industrial visit coordination, and global education programs. We tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each institution. Contact us through our inquiry form to discuss potential collaboration.

How do you ensure the quality of industrial visits and training programs?

We ensure quality through careful selection of industry partners, thorough pre-visit planning, structured learning objectives, experienced facilitators, and comprehensive post-visit assessments. Our programs are designed to align with curriculum goals while providing meaningful industry exposure.

Can enterprises customize workforce training programs?

Yes, our workforce upskilling solutions are fully customizable. We work closely with your organization to understand specific skill gaps and business objectives, then design training programs that address your unique requirements. Our solutions can range from technical skills development to leadership training.
